Instructions

Welcome to Homeless VR!

The objective of this game is to survive for 5 days. Each day is 20 seconds long.

You will beg as hard and genuine as you can. We will be the common people. Common people can provide free stuff if you beg well.

Free stuff include money for the shop and vitals.

Vitals include Thirst, Hunger, and Warmth. If any of them hits 0%, your health will start to lower. The health will reach 0% in 1 day, so act fast!

If your health is at 0%, you die.

Please wait for the game to start.
